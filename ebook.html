Successful recruitment process

If you are a recruiting manager or CEO of a startup then you know the importance of a well established process. A poor hiring process will not only waste your recruiter's time, but also the time of all the people involved in the process - both the developer - Tech Lead conducting technical interviews, and the Hiring Manager who is often the CEO of the company. In this ebook you will learn how to organize a good recruitment process, how to automate it, save time for your team and process only the best candidates that match your requirements.
