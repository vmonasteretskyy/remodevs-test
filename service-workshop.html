Our mission?

Training camp for junior recruiters

Due to the huge demand for IT recruiters, we decided to open a week-long boot camp for junior recruiters, during which they will not only learn the basics of recruiting and sourcing but will also be introduced to technologies used in IT and become independent recruiters in just a week of time.

The program includes:

  • Learning about sourcing - Boolean search and X-ray search
  • What characterizes an effective recruiter
  • What is Candidate Experience and why is it so important
  • What tools should be used in a recruiter's work
  • Learning about technology in IT
  • Learning about the Polish IT market
  • Creating candidate profiles
  • Creating effective job offers

And much more!
